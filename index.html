How it Works

Features

Pre-Qualified Agents Means You Start in a Better Place

You need as broad a representation of the market as possible to create the most competitive environment when quoting insurance policies. That is why Accusure has a pool of agents pre-qualified for your industry with diverse backgrounds and trusted insurance companies behind them. Never again do you need to spend time pre-qualifying agents.

Industry-Specific Customization

Your time is extremely valuable. That is why AccuSure helps you identify your industry type and then customizes the application to make it specifically relevant to that industry. This streamlines the back and forth communication between you and the agents so you can manage your business rather than your insurance.

Simple Communication with Agents

Need to respond potential agents, but can't find their email, fax or phone? With AccuSure, all the pertinent information is documented in a message. To save you even more time, you can communicate with all agents at the same time or individually.

Analyze Quotes Easily

Comparing quotes from various agents can be like trying to read a foreign language. With Accusure, we translate all quotes into your language. Agents are required to enter key information that you can view in an analyzer tool to determine the best choice for your company.

Learn Why and How to Make Things Easy

Remove the stress that normally comes with getting insurance quotes. We partner with you every step of the way while explaining how the process works and why certain information is necessary. Look for our application wizards throughout the application to support you throughout the process.

Keep It Organized with Workspace

AccuSure streamlines the cumbersome process of managing all your information. No longer do you have to worry about emails, phone calls and faxes. Your personal workspace will document all the activity and provide tools to easily move information to agents and view quotes.

FAQ

What is the cost?

Our service is COMPLETELY free to the business. No credit cards are required. EVER!

AccuSure ONLY charges a nominal fee to each agent that is matched to your specific Request for Insurance (RFI).

Why should I use AccuSure?

You need to focus on running your business NOT letting the insurance process run you. Have peace of mind that you have a partner to ensure you acquire the right insurance in the most cost effective manner possible.

Enter AccuSure. (1) Our web based software is free, safe and secure; (2) We pre-qualify the best agents in your area, specific to your industry; (3) Fill out only ONE simple application; (4) Then compare all your quotes using our analyzer tool

What is done with my information and how is it distributed?

AccuSure ONLY distributes your information to agents who we match to your business. These agents can ONLY use this information to correspond with Insurers to obtain quotes on your behalf. We do NOT sell your information to any other parties.

What is the difference between AccuSure and other online insurance websites?

AccuSure eliminates all the redundancies in an otherwise cumbersome and time consuming process. We partner with you by walking you through every step of the quoting process right down to analyzing the numbers. Our unique interactive application stores all your business information, communications and agent quotes in ONE convenient spot. NO other sites provide that.

Does AccuSure select agents for me?

YES, we select the agents for you. We match you with agents that service your specific area AND industry. You no longer have to spend hours trying to find an agent that delivers results based on your business profile.

How many agents will receive the information and how many quotes will I receive?

AccuSure will match you with enough agents to give you the broadest coverage of the insurance market. Typically, that number is 3 qualified agents. The number of quotes you receive will depend on your size, industry and risk characteristics. Agents will typically enter the best option, but may have alternative options they present to you as well.

Do I have to fill out an application?

Yes, but we have created a customized application specific for your industry that is easy and quick to fill out. We will also prepopulate information and class codes that are typical to your industry taking away some of the guess work for you. We also provide help bubbles/links throughout our system to explain and walk you through the process.

What else will the agents need other than my application?

Our application may be all that is necessary to obtain quotes. Of course, it all depends on the complexity and size of your business. Some insurance companies may need additional information. Our convenient message board allows you to respond to all agents so that you only have to answer a question once! Another time saver!

What is the process once my Request for Insurance is submitted?

You are matched to agents pre-qualified who service your geographic area with experience handling your industry type. We hold agents accountable to perform in accordance with our quality and professional standards. After they receive your information, agents will either provide you a quote or communicate if any additional information is needed.

How do I evaluate quotes?

AccuSure provides you a sophisticated quote analyzer tool so you can quickly compare ALL your quotes. We ensure all agents are quoting on the same criteria making the experience FULLY transparent to you. No longer do you have to feel like wondering if an agent has left something out. Only now can you feel confident that you are finally comparing apples to apples!

Can I upload documents such as safety plans, company financials, pictures of the buildings?

Yes, you can distribute this information to your assigned agents through our application where it is conveniently stored for you.

Can I still meet and/or speak with agents I meet through AccuSure?

You are in FULL control. AccuSure does not intend to replace the potentially valuable meetings between businesses and agents. If you like meeting or speaking with agents in person, feel free to interact with them in any way you wish. If you prefer not to have in person meetings or calls feel free to communicate with them from our message board.
